**Dataset Overview:**
The dataset comprises the following variables:

1. **Avg. Area Income:** The average income of residents in the area.
2. **Avg. Area House Age:** The average age of houses in the area.
3. **Avg. Area Number of Rooms:** The average number of rooms in houses in the area.
4. **Avg. Area Number of Bedrooms:** The average number of bedrooms in houses in the area.
5. **Area Population:** The population of the area.
6. **Price:** The target variable representing the price of the house.
7. **Address:** The address providing location information.
